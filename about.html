About Aaysha Trust

Dedicated to transparent Zakat collection and educational empowerment.

Who We Are

Aaysha Trust is a non‑profit organization committed to collecting and distributing Zakat in a transparent and Shariah‑compliant manner. Our primary focus is to support students who are unable to afford educational expenses due to financial hardship.

Why Aaysha Trust

  • 100% Education‑focused Zakat utilization
  • Strict student verification & document checks
  • Transparent donor & distribution records
  • Guided by Islamic principles and community advisors

Our Council

Our council consists of respected community members, educators, and Islamic scholars who oversee Zakat eligibility, verification, and ethical distribution.

Legal & Compliance Team

Our legal advisors ensure compliance with Indian laws, NGO regulations, and Income Tax provisions including 12A and 80G requirements (where applicable).

Our Process

  1. Students submit applications with documents
  2. Admin verifies background & references
  3. Eligible cases approved by council
  4. Zakat distributed directly for education
  5. Full transparency maintained for donors

How It Works

Donors contribute Zakat through our secure platform. Funds are allocated only to verified educational causes, ensuring your Zakat reaches deserving students with dignity and accountability.
