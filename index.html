Why Give Zakat?

PROPHET MUHAMMAD ﷺ SAID

مَا نَقَصَتْ صَدَقَةٌ مِنْ مَالٍ

“Zakat does not decrease wealth.”

When you give Zakat, your money does not reduce; instead, Allah puts blessing (barakah) in it.

(Sahih Muslim)

PROPHET MUHAMMAD ﷺ SAID

خَيْرُ النَّاسِ أَنْفَعُهُمْ لِلنَّاسِ

“The best of people are those who benefit others.”

A good person is not known by how much he has, but by how much he helps others.

(Al-Mu‘jam Al-Awsat)

ALLAH ﷻ SAYS IN THE QUR’AN

خُذْ مِنْ أَمْوَالِهِمْ صَدَقَةً تُطَهِّرُهُمْ

“Take charity from their wealth to purify them.”

Zakat cleans our wealth from greed and selfishness. It purifies our heart and helps society become fair and balanced.

(Qur’an 9:103)

Follow the guidance of the Qur’an and Sunnah to bring positive change in lives.
